CONSUMER AWARENESS ON PRODUCT LABELLING FOR IMPORTED FOOD


by

CAROLYNE FRED ,SANGEETHA A/P RAVINDRAN ,NORMAZNIZA HAZWANI BT ZULKAFLI 


                                                                                                                                ABSTRACT


This study aimed to investigate the consumer awareness on product labeling for imported food at Shah Alam, Selangor. This study was conducted at Giant Hypermarket and Tesco Stores ShahAlam, Selangor. Sample of 300 respondents selected from Giant Hypermarket and Tesco Stores customers. Apartfromthis, thestudyis also conducted to investigate the consumersâ€™ perception on the importance of reading food labeling and factors associated with awareness level to read food labels. Therefore, a set of questionnaire was used to obtain data. Data was analyzed using the "SPSS" version 20.0 (Statistical Package for Social Science). The results show that customers have high awareness level on food labeling and understand the information that found in food labeling. Safe to eat the product without reading the food labeling have lowest ranked. Based on the finding of this study, number of recommendations would be beneficial to the relevant authority, consumer association and manufacturers of local and imported product to improve the product labeling so consumer can understand easily when purchased.


Keywords: product labeling, consumer awareness, imported food.
