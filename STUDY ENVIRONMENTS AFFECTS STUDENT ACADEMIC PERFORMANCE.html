STUDY ENVIRONMENTS AFFECTS STUDENT ACADEMIC PERFORMANCE

by

AMER NADER BIN ABDUL JALIL, PUTERA ASCHRUFF ZAKARIA, MUHAMMAD NABEEL FIRDAUS .

                                                                                                                            ABSTRACT

Study environment affect student academic performance that determine what is the major environment that affect students. This study conducted to determine the major environment among students Politeknik Sultan Salahuddin Abdul Aziz Shah (PSA) and it includes all department. Besides, this study also aimed to the type of environment for all respondents related to their academic achievement and 360 respondents were involve in this study. A questionnaire consisting of 24 questions has been used in this study which is related to the environment that affect student academic performance. The result showed that the environment affect students grade in PSA which is involved in all department. 
Keywords: Environment, Study, Students, Performance
