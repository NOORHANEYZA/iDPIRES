ORGANIC FOOD- FACTORS INFLUENCE PURCHASE INTENTION AMONG CONSUMER IN SHAH ALAM

Article header with background image and parallax effect.

                                                                                                                              ABSTRACT

This study was conducted using descriptive research and the technique used was non-probability sampling and questionnaires were distributed to the respondents in the Shah Alam. Results shows factors of friendliness to environment influence consumers to purchase organic product highest compared to the other two factors which are safety and health aspects on organic products and availability of organic product information. This research was carried out whether the three factors friendliness to environment, safety and health, availability of information influence the purchasing intentions to the customers. This research was also to know whether demographic factors such as age, gender, marital status, education level influence consumers to purchase organic products. These were because of there was a growing interest in awareness of organic product among health conscious people around the world. In many industries recently, organic products are becoming increasingly vital and important in their everyday life. Furthermore, organic products was one the industries that people and government really look forward to promote.
The important in this research highlighted specifically on whether these three factors really influence consumers to purchase organic product as we know Malaysian are becoming more aware of their health conscious and governments were promoting people to live a healthy lifestyle.
The findings of the research determine the current situation in this society today. Therefore, this study is hoped to benefit all the parties involved in this industry. It was also hoped for the future research, this research will be useful as guideline and reference to other researchers to continue doing research about organic product.
Keywords : Environment, Organic, Customer, Health
