THE LEVEL OF AWARENESS ON ENTERPRENEURSHIP AMONG PSA & MSU STUDENTS

by

MOHAMAD FAZLEY BIN WAHYONO, NORSUZANAH BINTI SAMAT ,NURAZLIN BINTI SULAIMAN


                                                                                                                            ABSTRACT


In Malaysia the level of awareness of entrepreneurship among the fresh graduates are in average. That is a factor of the rise of unemployment in Malaysia. Due to this rising unemployment, it gave awareness to our fresh graduate to get involved in entrepreneurship in order to survive in a developing country. This study investigated the level of awareness among Politeknik Sultan Salahuddin Abdul Aziz Shah (PSA) and Management and Science University (MSU) students. The objective of this study is to determine the level of awareness of entrepreneurship among PSA and MSU students and to make comparison on level of awareness of entrepreneurship among PSA and MSU students. The instrument were used is questionnaire, it was given randomly to the students. The significance of this study is it can help the university to improve the practical training or any program that is related to entrepreneurship in other to improve studentâ€™s skills towards entrepreneurship. Besides that, this research can help the government to solve the problem of high unemployment rate because students now can be an entrepreneur with the skills that they have. 
Keywords: level of awareness, entrepreneurship, students
