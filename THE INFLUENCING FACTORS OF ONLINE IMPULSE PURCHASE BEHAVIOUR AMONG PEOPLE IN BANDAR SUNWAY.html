THE INFLUENCING FACTORS OF ONLINE IMPULSE PURCHASE BEHAVIOUR AMONG PEOPLE IN BANDAR SUNWAY

by

MOHD FAIZ BIN KAMAL BAHRIN, KHO SIEW LING, CHOONG XIN XIANG

                                                                                                                                  ABSTRACT


This research was conducted to determine the influencing factors of online impulse purchase behaviour among people in Bandar Sunway, the objectives of the research were: (i) to identify the consumers’ perception towards online impulse purchasing; (ii) to determine the factors that influence consumers’ impulse purchase behaviour through online shopping. Website attributes such as the website design, security and privacy, shopping services, and also shopping enjoyment have significant effect on consumers’ online impulsive purchasing behaviour. By understanding these website attributes, marketers can cater suitable needs to attract effective online marketing in the future. People with different income were found to have the similar impulse purchasing behaviour. This research was conducted to investigate the factors that influence consumers’ impulse purchase behaviour through online shopping. The sample in this study was a convenience list of 322 respondents randomly chosen from Bandar Sunway, Petaling Jaya, Selangor Darul Ehsan and there were 300 sets of usable questionnaires. Thus, this research paper will contribute towards a better understanding about online impulse purchasing.

Keyword: online shopping, purchase behaviour, website design, security and privacy, shopping services
