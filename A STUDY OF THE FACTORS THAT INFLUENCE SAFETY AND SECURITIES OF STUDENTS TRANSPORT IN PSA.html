A STUDY OF THE FACTORS THAT INFLUENCE SAFETY AND SECURITIES OF STUDENTS TRANSPORT IN PSA

BY

MUHD MUKHRIZ BIN ABD HAMID  , JANNATUL SHAZLEEN BINTI AZHARI ,NUR NAZIRAH BINTI PETER @ MOHD AZHAR 


ABSTRACT


The main purpose of this study is to identify the factors that influence of safety and securities of student transport at Politeknik Sultan Salahuddin Abdul Aziz Shah in Shah Alam. This study is to identify the perception student of safety and securities of student transport in Politeknik Sultan Salahuddin Abdul Aziz Shah. Research sample are chosen random involve 300 students from Diploma student in Electrical department, Mechanical department, Civil department and Commerce department that using their own transport to polytechnic. Data are collected by using instrument questionnaire, and has been analyse using SPSS. The significant of study is to identify the problems that this studentâ€™s faced.
