CUSTOMER PREFERENCES AND SATISFACTION TOWARDS COFFEE SHOP CHAINS IN SEC 13 SHAH ALAM

by

NUR ASILAH BINTI SALIM ,NURUL AMIRA BINTI AMIRULLAH ,‘ABIDAH BASIRAH BINTI SARAWAZAMAN 

                                                                                                                            ABSTRACT

The purpose of this research is to determine customer satisfaction towards coffee shop chains in Section 13, Shah Alam. This research can be used as guidelines for the coffee industry in their marketing decision due to improve in their performance in providing a better product or service to the customers. Coffee shops can also solve the problems occur resulting from aggressive competition thus enhance brand loyalty among consumers. A total of 150 questionnaires were distributed in Coffee Bean, Coffee Planet and Grind Roots Café at Section 13, Shah Alam through the method of simple random sampling. The research design used for this study is descriptive research. Data is collected through questionnaires (primary data) and secondary data (journal, books and internet references).  The result shows that all four factors influence consumer to choose coffee shop among the Shah Alam respondent. Factor Customer Service is the most important and significant factor that influence customer in choosing coffee shop chains.


 Keywords :Customer, Marketing, Services, Loyalty
