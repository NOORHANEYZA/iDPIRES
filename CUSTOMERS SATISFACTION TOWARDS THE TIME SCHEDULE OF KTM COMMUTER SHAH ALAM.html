                                                                                                                                 ABSTRACT


The paper reports studies on Customer satisfaction towards the time schedule of KTM Commuter that focuses on the respondent at Station Shah Alam. Additionally, it also attempts to measure the customer satisfaction level on the services provided by the KTM Commuter. The independent variable is the factors that involved the people that use the KTM Commuter as their daily transportation. The factors are effectiveness of the KTM Commuter. There are three objectives to be achieving which are to identify the customer satisfaction level toward time schedule KTM Commuter, to identify factors that cause delay on the time schedule of the KTM Commuter, to identify the effectiveness of the KTM Commuter services. The population for this research is 300 people as the respondent at KTM Commuter Station Shah Alam. The data was collected by distributing sets of questionnaire to the potential respondent and then the data was analyzed by using the Statistical Package for the Social Sciences for Windows (SPSS). The finding of the data was presented in a frequency and mean rate. The result of this research was showed that the factors in the independent variables are really influence the customer satisfaction toward the time delay of schedule by KTM Commuter.



Keywords: Customer, Transportation, Commuting, Public Transport
