CONSUMERS' RESPONSES TOWARDS POP-UP ADVERTISEMENT IN MOBILE PHONE IN PSA

by

NUR ASYILLAH AISYAH BINTI IDRES ,SARAH SHAHIRAH BINTI MOHD AZAHAR ,NUR AZILA BINTI RUSLIN

                                                                                                                            ABSTRACT

First and foremost, the actual purpose of the study is to study the consumer’s responses towards pop-up advertisement in mobile phone in Polytechnic Shah Alam. Data was obtained through self-administered questionnaire. Based on the study, researchers found out that there were three variables that affecting consumers’ responses which is irritation, informativeness and credibility. The three variables will be evaluate and studied deeper as consumers will be answering the questionnaires regarding pop-up advertisement in mobile phone. The sample in this study was a convenience sampling 351 respondents randomly chooses from staff and student in Polytechnic Shah Alam. Results of this research are favourable and will help to explain the consumer’s responses towards pop-up advertisement in mobile phone among staff and student in Polytechnic Shah Alam. The research paper will contribute towards a better understanding about the consumer’s responses towards pop-up advertisement in mobile phone.


Keywords : Consumer, advertisement, mobile
