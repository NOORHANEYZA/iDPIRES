STUDY ON CLEANLINESS OF NIGHT MARKET
FOOD SELLING IN SECTION 18, SHAH ALAM.

by

MOHAMAD ASWADI BIN ISMAIL ,NORHASHIMAH BINTI MOHD ISMAIL ,AISYAH FARHAN BINTI ABDUL JALAL

                                                                                                                            ABSTRACT

The purpose of this study is to study the cleanliness of night market food selling in Section 18, Shah Alam. Base on the study, the researchers investigate the cleanliness at Section 18 night market by approaching three group of respondent which is food sellers at Section 18, resident at Section 18 and authorities who responsible in the cleanliness at Section 18 night market. Pilot test was conducted on 30 buyers at Section 18 night market to test respondent understanding on the questionnaire.

Data collected by using questionnaire, interview and observation. The respondents that involve in this study are from different background such as age, gender and origin. Actual questionnaire was conducted on 370 residents at Section 18, Shah Alam which represent the 10000 resident there. In addition, the internal consistency using Cronbash Alpha also used to verify the measurement that will be use later. An expected result is to find out the cleanliness of night market food selling at Section 18, Shah Alam.


Keywords : Environment, Cleanliness, Authorities
